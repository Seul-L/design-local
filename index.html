Connecting the dots.

Design.Local is a network of design thinkers from a variety of disciplines in KW that are collaborating to solve problems for small business initiatives to make stronger communities.

Our community, our team.

Our region includes businesses both large and small, and we rely on them to help drive our economy. By competing both locally and globally and employing people in our community, each business has an impact on the strength of our society by virtue of their ability to thrive. As much as competition drives growth, collaboration and connectedness to others is just as important in driving our economy forward.

Photo Credit: Jason Paris (cc-by-2.0)

Our Focus

With the exponential growth of easily available technology, the potential for economic growth is improved. However, not all businesses and institutions have the ability to utilize modern systems, platforms, processes, or tools to their best potential, either because they don’t have the know-how, resources, or access to people with the necessary skills.

Who' s Involved

Designers

Academia

Government

Our Municipal Government has the goal to develop and execute on activities that make our communities thrive.

Imagine the Possibilities

When design thinkers come together to solve problems that are meaningful for businesses in our community that need it most, great things can happen.

Connecting the Dots

This is not a political campaign speech, and this is certainly not charity work. On the contrary, Design.local is connecting the dots by bringing people together in a truly innovative way.
