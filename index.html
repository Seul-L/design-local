Connecting the dots.

We’re an autonomous community of diverse designers and problem solvers collaborating in an innovative way to help make stronger communities.

Our community, our team.

Our region includes businesses both large and small, and we rely on these businesses to drive our economy by employing our people, and by remaining competitive. You can think of our local economy as out team. Strong teams not only encourage healthy competition they also look for ways to work together to take advantage of their strengths, and to look for opportunities to be even better. Design.local has a bold vision, and sees an opportunity where we can leverage a strength to help our community be even better.

Photo Credit: Jason Paris (cc-by-2.0)

Where we can look to improve.

Technology and innovation is available, and they will only continue to develop at exponential rates, we get that and certainly appreciate the promises and potential that they deliver. However, we also know that there are businesses and institutions that don’t utilize modern systems, platforms, processes, or tools to their best potential. This is either because they don’t have the know-how, resources, or access to people with the necessary skills on a full or even part-time basis.

Who can help us

Designers

We have a unique collection of skilled people across multiple discplines and industries in our community, each with their own insight into solving problems.

Academia

We have world-class post-secondary schools that put out new cohorts of people (problem solvers, designers, and thinkers) every year that we would like to see stay here in the region.

Government

We have a Municipal Government with the goal to develop and execute on the activities that help to make our communities a thriving place to live.

Imagine the Possibilities

When designers and thinkers come together for even a short amount of time to solve problems that are meaningful for businesses in our community that need it the most.

We see this opportunity too.

This is not a political campaign speech, and this is certainly not charity work. On the contrary, Design.local is connecting the dots by bringing people together in a truly innovative way.
