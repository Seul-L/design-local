Youth Council at THEMUSEUM

Youth Council at THEMUSEUM is a group of 13-17 year olds who meet monthly to share their diverse voices and create a project for youth by youth.
THEMUSEUM is a registered not for profit charity based in downtown Kitchener. Their mission is to scan the globe for fresh cultural content and use it to stage experiences that stimulate transformative connections for the audiences.

Youth engagement strategy

Current situation

We are looking for a way to contribute our efforts towards climate change. Our idea is for the kids to complete tasks/missions that were positive for climate change, and make it an interactive fun game of sorts.

Goals

Kids to be interacting with the solution that results in an action on climate change with us.

Users

13-17 year old highschool students who meet monthly at THEMUSEUM.

Proposed Idea

Perhaps we could create a website or online platform where people could log on and complete missions/tasks, upload photos and track their points and progress.

Other Notes

Other This project is tied to the upcoming exhibition at THEMUSEUM called ALARM which will be open from January-June 2020.
