Design Challenges Brief

Artist Centre-Artist Connection

How can we connect artists and organizations in specifically Waterloo Region, to create an artist-run centre? Alternatively a one source of Tribal Knowledge for Artists to access resources.

Branding & Seal stamp Design

Current situation

There are so many organizations acting independently, how can we bring everyone together, or create something new and innovative in the face of COVID 19?

Artists are part of the lowest-paid demographic. They are also the most innovative. However, Kitchener Waterloo is one of the only places in Canada that does not have an artist-run centre yet we are one of the wealthiest cities.

Goal

The goal of this project is to bring artists, organizations and resources together under one umbrella, increase more collaboration and less competition.

Users

Users who we identified are:

  • Artists
  • Art organizations
  • Community organizations
  • Schools

The pain points are:

  • There is a disconnect between multiple organizations and creatives
  • There are siloed organizations that are disconnected

Success Matrix

  • There is an uptake within the Artistic community
  • Multiple organizations partner together
  • Increase in artist visibility

As a result of this project, users would have the ability to create more work, more meaningful work and gain more visibility. Also, non-native people from Kitchener-Waterloo will have more access and be able to integrate into the community with a smoother transition.

Other Notes

  • This is not the first time the issue of an artist-run centre has been tackled. There was a round table and there are other non-profits and attempts. There are issues with longevity and uptake.
