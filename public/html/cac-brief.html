Design Challenges Brief

Cellular Agriculture Canada

Cellular Agriculture Canada is a nationwide interdisciplinary organization with the mission to support and promote the cellular agriculture industry and research in Canada.

Website strategy

Current situation

One of our biggest challenges is keeping our website up to date with limiting resources. We have a "Resources" section that needs to be updated continuously, but we have not been able to do it due to lack of people and time. Currently we collect information from news and social media, and update on the “resource” section of the website.

Goal

We want to keep our website to keep updated so that we can reduce time spent on researching and updating the website.

Users

Primarily admin and other volunteers would use the result of this project. In terms of the website, the user is hard to define, maybe General public and companies who are interested? But we want to focus on youth (Millennials, GenZ) to visit our website.

The main pain point for admin and volunteers is limited time. There is a learning curve and restrictions on Squarespace. We spend a lot of time searching for suitable articles. But we have around 5 people spending 3-8 hours per week on the entire organization so we are not sure how much time we should spend on updating the website either.

Principles

We want the solution to be simple and quick that results in reducing time spent on research and updating on the website. If possible, I want more traffic to the website as well.

Other Notes

We use squarespace and our budget is very low. We expect to have some kind of strategy (may be possible automation strategy) and implementation process document at the end of the project.

Summary

We’ve set out to accomplish creating a website update strategy for volunteers and admins where the solution will be simple and quick.
