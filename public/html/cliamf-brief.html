Design Challenges Brief

Canadian Low Income Artist & Musician Fund

Canadian Low Income Artist & Musician Fund (CLIAMF) is a campaign created and organized by 2 individuals who want to provide support for low-income artists and musicians whose livelihoods are being affected by this pandemic.
Whether it's from cancelled gigs, lost jobs, or a lack of business due to coronavirus scares, our intention for this fund is to collectively raise money in order to provide emergency and preventative resources to those at financial risk until our government puts a solid plan in action to help.

Website strategy

Current situation

In order to raise the targeted goal of $5000 CAD, CLIAMF (Canadian Low Income Artist & Musician Fund) needs to raise awareness and encourage action towards their cause.
Currently the organization is running off of a spreadsheet, and a go-fund-me page but needs help putting together a website landing page, a social media strategy, and a network of advocates who can help promote this great cause.

Goal

Increase donations engagement, build online brand presence, and reach the target goal of $5000 CAD in donations. Some actionable items include:

  • Create a way for people to learn more about this organization and ultimately donate to the cause
  • Create a social media/outreach strategy to help raise awareness for this cause
  • Fail fast - we want to ideate, build, test, measure, and repeat as efficiently as possible in order to get to a working solution as quickly as possible.

Users

The user is yet to be determined until the design challenge is underway and the results have been reviewed. The stakeholders involved with this project include:

  • Musicians, Artists, Performers
  • Galleries, Theater, Venues (Restaurants)
  • Administrative staff
  • Service industry and gig work- (lowest income bracket)

Scenarios

Currently, there is a gofundme page that users can visit to learn more about this initiative but they need support in terms of getting a digital strategy in place.

Principles

We need a low-cost solution that is simple and can be managed by the two people who are managing this initiative.
